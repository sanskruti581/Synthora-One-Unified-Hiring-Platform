# Synthora.AI

Synthora.AI is an AI-powered recruitment platform designed to make hiring faster, more consistent, and more insight-driven. The project focuses on automating the complete candidate evaluation journey, from candidate intake and job description analysis to interview rounds, scoring, ranking, and final hiring reports.

## Why Synthora.AI

Hiring teams often spend a large amount of time on repetitive screening, interview coordination, manual evaluation, and comparison of candidates. These steps can slow down hiring drives and may lead to inconsistent decisions when different interviewers evaluate candidates differently.

Synthora.AI addresses this by providing a unified platform where AI helps organizations evaluate candidates using structured, role-specific signals. It reduces manual effort while giving recruiters and hiring managers clearer data for shortlisting the right talent.

## What the Project Does

Synthora.AI automates and supports the major stages of recruitment:

- Candidate profile and resume intake
- Job description based evaluation setup
- AI-driven aptitude, technical, coding, and HR interview rounds
- Consistent candidate scoring and ranking
- Skill, communication, confidence, and role-fit analysis
- Recruiter-ready reports with final shortlist recommendations

The goal is not only to reduce screening time, but also to improve the quality and fairness of hiring decisions by using consistent evaluation criteria across all candidates.

## Core Idea

The platform acts as a smart hiring assistant for organizations. Recruiters provide candidate information and role requirements, and Synthora.AI uses that context to conduct structured assessments. Each candidate is evaluated across relevant hiring dimensions, then ranked with clear insights that help the hiring team make faster and better decisions.

## Key Features

### AI Interviews

Synthora.AI supports automated interview rounds such as aptitude, technical, coding, and HR conversations. These interviews are designed to evaluate both knowledge and communication in a structured way.

### End-to-End Hiring Automation

The project brings multiple hiring steps into one flow. Instead of handling screening, interviews, scoring, and reporting separately, Synthora.AI connects them into a single recruitment process.

### Advanced Candidate Analytics

The platform focuses on data-backed insights such as skill match, confidence, communication quality, technical ability, and role fit. This helps recruiters compare candidates beyond basic resume keywords.

### Candidate Ranking and Shortlisting

After evaluation, candidates are scored and ranked so hiring teams can quickly identify top profiles. The final output is intended to support practical decisions, not just display raw scores.

### Secure and Reliable Workflow

Because hiring involves sensitive candidate information, the project emphasizes professional-grade data handling, reliable workflows, and controlled access patterns.

## How Synthora.AI Helps

Synthora.AI is useful for companies, recruiters, placement teams, and hiring managers who need to handle many candidates efficiently. It is especially valuable when hiring drives require consistent evaluation across large candidate pools.

It helps by:

- Reducing repetitive recruiter workload
- Saving time spent on manual screening
- Improving consistency across interviews
- Supporting faster shortlisting decisions
- Giving clearer candidate comparison data
- Making large-scale hiring more manageable
- Reducing interviewer fatigue during repeated evaluation rounds

## Typical Hiring Flow

1. Candidates are uploaded with their resumes or profile details.
2. The job description is added to define role expectations.
3. Synthora.AI prepares evaluation criteria based on the role.
4. AI interview agents conduct structured assessment rounds.
5. Each candidate receives a score based on relevant hiring signals.
6. The platform ranks candidates according to role fit.
7. Recruiters receive a final report with insights and recommendations.

## Project Vision

The vision of Synthora.AI is to create a unified hiring platform where AI does the repetitive evaluation work while humans stay focused on final judgment, team fit, and strategic hiring decisions.

By combining automation, structured interviews, analytics, and reporting, Synthora.AI aims to make recruitment faster, smarter, and more reliable for modern hiring teams.
